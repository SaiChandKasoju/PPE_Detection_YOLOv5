PPE Detection on Construction Sites

Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) is vital for ensuring safety on construction sites. Helmets, vests, and face masks protect workers from hazards such as falling objects, dust, or chemical exposure. Manual PPE inspection can be time-consuming and error-prone. This system leverages a YOLOv5l object detection model trained with custom annotations to automatically detect PPE compliance across images, videos, or live feeds.
